Content Writer

Content Writer

We are looking for a passionate, creative, and results-oriented advertising Content Writer. A successful candidate will have polished writing skills, be able to relate, understand, and connect with a multitude of online users, and have an aptitude for online marketing across platforms and audiences. If you’re great at working autonomously as well as with cross-functional teams, and you’re interested in having a direct impact on the bottom line, this is the opportunity for you. The ideal candidate will be able to produce short- form ad copy and longer-form articles for any vertical.

Day-to-Day Responsibilities

  • Research content topics and vertical themes, review creative strategies, and develop content
  • Create content for different websites and advertising networks using internal and third-party tools
  • Work directly with media buyers to understand performance content needs
  • Continually adjust and fine-tune content and copy to appropriately target a wide array of content with our intended end users
  • Assess content performance in combination with marketing campaigns, analyzing performance metrics and financial results
  • Communicate and report on content findings and recommend actions to ensure achievement of performance goals
  • Explore the value of new creative opportunities within our team’s specific workflows

Needed Skills

  • 1-3 years of content writing/copywriting experience
  • Able to effectively multi-task in a deadline-driven environment and meet tight deadlines
  • Superior work ethic and ability to produce a large amount of high quality content in many different verticals - from Facebook ads to longer form articles
  • Critical thinking ability and attention to detail
  • Outstanding writing, editing, proofreading, grammar, spelling, and punctuation skills
  • Experience in online marketing on social media and native networks, like Taboola and Outbrain
  • B.A. in English, Journalism, Creative Writing, or other writing-intensive Humanities degree a plus

Contract Writer

Contract Writer

Qool Media is currently looking for contract writers who are passionate, knowledgeable, and experienced content creators. We work with writers who have strong research skills and a knack for knowing what online audiences want to read.

Specifically, we are hiring specialist writers for the following verticals: senior, health, auto, technology, and finance. Applicants should ideally have experience writing in at least one of the topics above.

Our expected turnaround time is 7 days, but we are open to negotiating different terms.

Qualifications and Requirements

  • A minimum of 3 published articles with your name in the byline
  • Strong research skills
  • Ability to meet agreed upon deadlines
  • Contribute entirely original, previously unpublished work
  • Follow guidelines provided in content briefings
  • Adapt writing style to match the voice of our sites

Please note that we do not accept unsolicited articles. We also do not publish guest posts or any other material that is sent to us unasked. Only submit articles that have been asked of you to write. Additionally, we expect all submitted work to be original, we have a zero-tolerance policy on plagiarism.

If we like your application we will reach out to you to discuss writing a trial article for one of our sites. All trial articles are compensated and will be negotiated with you ahead of time.

We look forward to hearing from you!

Data Quality Analyst

Data Quality Analyst

Qool Media is looking for a Data Quality Analyst to join our Data Engineering team. The Data Engineering team is the horizontal layer that supports business intelligence, optimization, machine learning, and external & internal reporting. We process and report on hundreds of millions of events and user attributes per day, gathered from an extremely heterogeneous set of data streams.As a Data Quality Analyst in the Data Engineering team, you will be working with engineering teams across System1 & Qool Media to ensure that we have the highest quality of data in and out of the platform.Attention to detail and a desire to learn are key to this position.Our database systems include Snowflake, Postgres, Redshift, and MS SQL server. We also utilize a range of technologies including Kinesis, Spark, and Redis.

Day-to-Day Responsibilities

  • Identify data anomalies and analyze data trends across our inbound/outbound data feeds and data warehouses
  • Work with the engineers on the team to have these checks automated
  • Triage incoming requests to determine severity and root cause
  • Assist the BI, Data Science, and Marketing teams with data investigations

Qualifications

  • Bachelors degree (technical or science) preferred
  • Strong attention to detail
  • 1+ years working in SQL (Postgres/MySQL) in a production environment
  • Knowledge of basic Python scripting or a desire to learn

Digital Media Buyer (Search/Social)

  • Identify high value demographics and target them accordingly
  • Develop a high level of digital marketing and search engine marketing expertise

Full-Stack Software Developer

Full-Stack Software Developer

Qool Media is looking for a Full-Stack Software Developer to join our Publishing team to help us build next generation marketing platforms for the social and search channels. This role is a full-time position located at our office in Guelph, ON.

The ideal candidate is self-motivated and interested in doing quality work in a dynamic and collaborative environment. Flexibility, a drive to have an impact, a knack for solving tough engineering problems, and the ability to collaborate and communicate effectively are keys to success in this position. If you like to develop cloud services, process large volumes of data, and work on optimization algorithms, then you will enjoy working with us.

Working within the Publishing team, you’ll:

  • Own individual software development lifecycles; meet with stakeholders to obtain project specifications; deliver on client and product manager expectations
  • Write production quality code from scratch to deployment
  • Use personal experience and insight to build out new features, using technologies that are best suited to the job
  • Maintain and improve upon our platforms currently coded using .NET, MSSQL, and AngularJS
  • Utilize the latest technologies such as .NET Core, Docker, Snowflake, and Angular
  • Have end-to-end ownership of systems and services; design, develop, and launch services; have thorough understanding of the entire system

You have:

  • BS in Computer Science or equivalent
  • 3-4 years’ experience writing and shipping code
  • Strong understanding of object-oriented programming and related principles, and service- oriented architectures
  • Proficient experience with .NET, C#, and MSSQL is a must
  • Proficiency with Angular is a plus
  • Experience with AWS is a plus
  • Experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is a plus
  • Familiarity with Agile methodologies, domain driven design, and TDD is a plus
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills

Software QA Analyst

Software QA Analyst

Our software development teams have grown quickly in 2019 and as we prepare for the new year we’re making new investments in maintaining our high quality bar in our applications. With the need for a dedicated testing and QA team, we are ready to begin expanding this initiative to include an additional QA Analyst at Qool Media.

Our QA Analyst will be responsible for supporting our dedicated QA tester to ensure that our applications are proactively assessed for bugs and inefficiencies. Together they will work with our front and back end teams on a variety of applications built with leading technologies such as .NET Core, PHP, Angular, SQL, and Javascript.

Key Responsibilities

  • Share expertise in design of the product as well as business cases to better support testing.
  • Define and deliver automated scripts for the product under test.
  • Provide clear and precise reports on testing coverage, progress and defects.
  • Participate in all facets of the agile process.
  • Communicate preemptively about any road blocks for your/ teams deliverables to clear hurdles.

Desired Qualifications

  • 3-5 years working in a similar role within software quality assurance
  • Experience working within an Agile/Scrum team environment
  • Highly developed organizational and time management skills
  • Demonstrated experience with testing Web Applications and Javascript
  • Demonstrated understanding of relational databases and SQL
  • Knowledge of defect management and reporting techniques
  • Excellent analytical skills and commitment to continuous testing improvement
  • Experience with all phases and stages of testing desired (Functional, System, Integration, Data Validation, User Acceptance, Performance, Stress, Load)
  • Experience designing and building Automation test scripts, test infrastructure and tools for both frontend and backend testing
  • Experience with Mabl Automation tool is a plus
  • Familiarity with WordPress, C#, or Angular is a plus
